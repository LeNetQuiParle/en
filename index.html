About

I am a scratcher passionate about programming, robotics (everything related to computing), and mathematics. I love creating games and applications to simplify my life, have fun, distract myself or just to pass the time. I program with several programming languages ​​besides scratch, including:

Why Le_PC_qui_parle ?

I chose the pseudonym "Le_PC_qui_parle" because I like computing (Le_PC) and I am a human (qui_parle). In french, "PC" is a computer and "qui parle" would say "talking" (So it's "The_talking_computer").
